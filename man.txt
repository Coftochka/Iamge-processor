usage:
    image_proceessor input_file.bmp output_file.bmp [-options]
        or 
    image_proceessor --help

options:
--help              To print this manual
-gs                 To use Grayscale filter
-sharp              To make image shearper and more detalized
-edge threshold     To find edges of objects with given sensivity
-crop width height  To crop image to size of (width, height), counted from top left corner
